Clusterify lets you propose small coding projects and team up with others to realize them.
Join Projects >>
  • Do the project live over chat, each in your own time or even meet in person.
  • Vote for ideas & completed projects you like. Showcase your work on your blog or elsewhere online.
  • Use the tools you want, administer the project the way you want
Clusterify is great for:
  • ⇒ Programming events
    • Planning a 2-3 hours "code anything" session? Have attendees exchange ideas & team up in advance to maximize coding time on-site.
  • ⇒ Freelancers, startup founders
    • Add variety to your portfolio and find potential work partners. Find out if you work well with them by working on a real (yet short) project.
  • ⇒ Open source projects
    • Propose short tasks to be done on the project to get people interested and involved.
  • ⇒ Hobby coding projects
    • Find other coders who feel the same need or like your idea.
  • ⇒ Learning new languages
    • A Clusterify project is a great context to share tips and learn along with others.
